# Commercial License — Vibe Payroll Time

The source in this repository is offered under the
[PolyForm Internal Use 1.0.0](./LICENSE) license, which permits free use for
**internal business purposes** — for example, a CPA firm running Vibe Payroll
Time to track the hours of its own staff.

A **commercial license** is required to use Vibe Payroll Time in any of the
following scenarios:

- **Client-portal use** — hosting client companies on the appliance so that
  the client's employees punch in, the client's supervisors approve time, and
  the client receives payroll exports.
- **Reselling or redistributing** the software (as-is or modified) to third
  parties.
- **Offering the functionality of the software as a service**, either
  internally across unrelated organizations or externally to any third party.

## Tiers

Commercial tiers mirror Vibe MyBooks:

- **Per-firm unlimited annual** — priced by total employee seats across all
  companies under the firm's appliance.
- **Per-firm capped annual** — priced by company count cap.
- **Per-company monthly** — for small businesses running a dedicated
  appliance.

Licenses are issued through the shared `kisaes-license-portal` at
